RHF0M003

RisingHF LoRaWAN® module based on RHF0M003 chip. It is a low cost, low power and ultra-small size module, embedded with Semtech’s LoRa® propriety chip SX127x and ST’s ultra-low power MCU STM32L07x. RHF0M003 LoraWAN® module is targeted application at wireless sensor network， AMR, wearable devices and others IOT devices powered by battery which need low power consumption to extend the battery life.
RHF0M003 key features are listed below, details can be found into datasheet:

  • Supply voltage: 3.3V;
  • Operating voltage: 3.3V;
  • Low power consumption: 1.4uA sleep current in WOR mode;
  • Dual band design:
    • Low frequency band (LF20): 20 dBm at 434MHz/470MHz;
    • High frequency band (HF20): 20 dBm at 868MHz/915MHz;
  • User-friendly interface:
    • AT commands (documentations here and here);
    • LPUART/USART/I2C/USB, 2 × ADC，10 × GPIOs;
  • Support LoRaWAN® protocol;
